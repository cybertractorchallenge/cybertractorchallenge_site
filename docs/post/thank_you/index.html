Thank you to our 2024 CyberTractor Challenge Sponsors!

By CyberTractor Challenge

June 11, 2024

We would like to extend our heartfelt gratitude to our 2024 CyberTractor Challenge sponsors. Our sponsors share our commitment to fostering a more secure agricultural industry by educating the next generation of agricultural & cybersecurity professionals with the knowledge and skills necessary to tackle the unique cybersecurity challenges facing modern farming.

Our Sponsors

This year, we are proud to partner with the following sponsors whose support and commitment to our cause helps us achieve our goals. Their sponsorship is a testament to their dedication to creating a safer, more secure agricultural industry.

  • John Deere: Platinum Sponsorship
  • CNH Industrial: Gold Sponsorship
  • AGCO: Silver Sponsorship

Bronze and Other Contributions

  • Checkmarx
  • BAWLS Guarana

How Sponsor Support Makes a Difference

Thanks to the generous contributions of our sponsors, we are able to provide our students with world-class cybersecurity educational opportunities.

Here’s how their support makes a difference:

  • Educational Programs: Funding enables us to offer this unique, invaluable training program which covers the latest in agricultural and industry cybersecurity practices.
  • Student Scholarships: Financial support allows us to provide travel, room, and board at no cost to students, ensuring that financial barriers do not prevent passionate individuals from participating.
  • Industry Collaboration: Our sponsors bridge the gap between academia, industry, competitors, and cybersecurity experts by fostering collaboration and supporting new innovations.

Celebrate!

We invite you to join us in celebrating these exciting partnerships. Stay tuned for updates on our event and opportunities to get involved. Together, we are building a brighter and more secure future for the agricultural industry.

For more information about the 2024 CyberTractor Challenge and how you can support our mission, please visit our website cybertractorchallenge.org.

Thank you again to our sponsors and supporters for making this possible!
