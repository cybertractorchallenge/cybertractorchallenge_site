Thank You to Our 2025 CyberTractor Challenge Sponsors!

By CyberTractor Challenge

June 26, 2025

The 2025 CyberTractor Challenge set new standards with expanded classes, advanced technical content, and a stronger community. Thanks to our outstanding sponsors and supporters, this year demonstrated our growth and potential. We aimed to create a more impactful CyberTractor Challenge, and our sponsors’ support made this vision a reality.

[Upstream](https://upstream.auto/) for supporting our students and this event with amazing swag and interactions with our students and other sponsors

How Sponsor Support Makes a Difference

This year’s challenge offered comprehensive hands-on experience. We introduced new classes and integrated practical scenarios that pushed students to think critically:

  • Expanded Curriculum: Added instruction in embedded systems development and AI attack surfaces, giving students insight into security and agricultural technology integration.
  • Access for Everyone: All costs covered for students and educators, including travel, housing, and food.
  • Mentorship and Collaboration: Industry experts worked directly with students and educators, building lasting professional connections.

More Than a Sponsor

Special thanks to Iowa State University for hosting the 2025 CyberTractor Challenge. Your facilities and support were essential to our success. We also thank the DigitalAg team for contributing their time and expertise. For those interested in cybersecurity or advanced degrees in a variety of fields, ISU offers a great opportunity to grow your skills.

Academic Partnership Highlight

We extend our appreciation to Colorado State University and Dr. Jeremy Daily for their continued expertise and dedication. Jeremy has been active in this event since its inception as both a planner and an educator. For those considering graduate studies in systems engineering or cybersecurity, CSU is an excellent option. Contact Dr. Daily at jeremy[.]daily[@]colostate[.]edu for more information.

Thank you again to our sponsors, students, teachers, mentors, volunteers, and all who donated their time for making 2025 our most successful year yet. We look forward to our 5th annual CyberTractor Challenge in 2026.

If you’d like to support next year’s CyberTractor Challenge or learn more about our work, visit cybertractorchallenge.org.

See you next year, Tractor Hackers
