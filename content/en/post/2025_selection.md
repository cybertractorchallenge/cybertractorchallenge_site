# 2025 CyberTractor Challenge Student Selections Completed!

The CyberTractor Challenge team is excited to announce that student selections for the 2025 CyberTractor Challenge have been completed! We are thrilled to welcome a diverse group of talented students from across the country who will be joining us in June for this unique opportunity to learn, collaborate, and innovate in the field of agriculture cybersecurity. The CyberTractor Challenge exists to develop talent and build community to solve hard and interesting problems related to the security of modern connected agricultural equipment.

Being this is the FOURTH offering of the CyberTractor Challenge, its reach and impact have been expanded. It will be held 16-20 June 2025 at the Hansen Center on the campus of Iowa State University in Ames Iowa. The CyberTractor Challenge board and volunteers are grateful to this year's event sponsors for their support: John Deere, CASEIH, AGCO, and others. Their commitment to fostering the next generation of cybersecurity professionals is invaluable. 

If you are interested in learning more about the CyberTractor Challenge, please visit the [website](https://www.cybertractorchallenge.org) for more information.
